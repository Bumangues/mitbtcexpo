What we'll do

The MIT Bitcoin Expo will contain a wide range of industry panels and talks from top leaders in the blockchain space, as well as opportunities to meet like minded enthusiasts, developers, entrepreneurs and innovators.

Come join us for our first Women in Blockchain networking happy hour after day 1 of the MIT Bitcoin Expo. Speakers joining us at the happy hour:

  • Amy Yin - Software Engineer, Coinbase
  • Bettina Warburg - Co-founder, Animal Ventures
  • Elaine Shi - Associate Professor, Cornell
  • Jay Graber - Developer, Zcash
  • Jill Carlson - Independent Consultant
  • Lucianna Kiffer - PhD Student, Northeastern University
  • Preethi Kasireddy - Blockchain Engineer
  • Rebekah Mercer - Cryptography, Researcher
  • Sharon Goldberg - CEO, Commonwealth Crypto
  • Stefanie Roos - Postdoc, University of Waterloo
