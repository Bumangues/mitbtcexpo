About

Join the MIT Bitcoin Expo in building our decentralized future. We will be bringing together 500 developers, hackers, and industry expert from around the world to compete in a 24-hour hackathon to build the best decentralized technology for Web 3.0. This year, the MIT Bitcoin Hackathon will be taking place March 16-17 at the Ray and Maria Stata Center. We'll be providing hackers with free food, travel reimbusements, prizes, and opportunities to present their projects to top industry judges at the annual MIT Bitcoin Expo.

Get ready to push forward the future of development.

Prizes:
Grand Prize: $15,000
Second Place: $10,000
Third Place: $5,000
